Welcome to Georgia Tech's Mixed Reality Club!

About

Enable GT students to build mixed reality apps and understand the long-term potential of mixed reality technology in the real world.

To make the use of the latest, cutting edge hardware and publish applications for use on campus and in the industry. Using high-quality hardware to deploy mixed reality apps for campus and industry use.

To enable members to understand both the technology used to build AR/VR products and contextualize this knowledge in the real-world by connecting them with industry mentors in this space. Connecting GT students with industry experts and startup founders for guidance in the mixed reality sector.
